Our Journey

With over 50 years of experience, a present and a future that it cultivates and capitalizes with a dynamic of progression and development, BH Bank is considered today as one of the main players in economic activity in Tunisia.

Timeline

1974

Creation of the National Housing Savings Fund.

1989

Transformation of the CNEL into a Housing Bank.

1993

BH was made up of financial, real estate and technological centers.

2005-2009

Continental Reputation through awards such as Best African Bank for Mortgage Credit.

2014

New vision and new approach aimed at performance and taking the path of rigor and excellence.

2019

Transformation of the Housing Bank into BH Bank.

BH Bank began to reap the benefits of its development strategy in various areas, which remains its driver of sustainable development and performance.
